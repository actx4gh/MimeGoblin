MimeGoblin

Routes downloads into subfolders based on regex rules. Paths are always relative to your Downloads directory.

Template tokens

You can use tokens like ${filename}, ${ext}, ${host}, ${date}, ${yyyy}, ${mm}, ${dd}.

For capture groups, use ${filename:1} (or ${url:2}, etc.) where the prefix matches the "On" field for that rule.

Folder templates are sanitized: no absolute paths, no .., no leading slashes.

Recent routing (local)

This is stored locally (not synced) and limited to 50 entries by default.

Note: Chrome only allows extensions to suggest paths relative to your configured Downloads directory.
